# ChartedX                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
ChartedX is a desktop trading journal designed to help traders track, analyze, and improve their performance.                                                                                                                               

Log your trades, monitor your P&L, review your win rate and profit factor, and identify patterns in your trading with charts and a calendar view. Charted also calculates a **Charted Score** — a performance rating based on your overall
trading consistency.

Built for serious traders who want clarity on their results.
